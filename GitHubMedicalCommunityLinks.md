# GitHub Medical Community Links

## Purpose

This document maps open-source projects that may provide building blocks, modules, research foundations or inspiration for the MedBay platform.

The goal is not to collect repositories by popularity or activity. The goal is to identify useful medical capabilities that can contribute to MedBay.

## Evaluation Model

### MedBay Relevance

★★★★★ Direct module candidate

★★★★ Strong candidate

★★★ Research foundation

★★ Inspiration

★ Currently outside scope
